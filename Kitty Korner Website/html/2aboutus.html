Know Our Story

Welcome to Kitty Korner,

your one-stop shop for all kinds of cats!

We are a family-owned and operated business that has been breeding and selling cats for over 20 years. We are passionate about cats and we believe that they make wonderful companions. We offer a wide variety of cats to choose from, including pure breeds and mixed breeds. We also have a variety of ages and colors to choose from. All of our cats are healthy and well-socialized. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service. If you are looking for a new furry friend, we encourage you to visit our website today. We are confident that you will find the perfect cat for your family.

Our Vision


  • To provide the highest quality cats and cat care products to our customers.
  • To create a positive and welcoming environment for our customers and their cats.
  • To be a resource for cat lovers, providing information and education about cat care and behavior.
  • To support the local cat community, through donations to shelters and rescue organizations.
  • To make a difference in the lives of cats and their humans, by providing them with the love and care they deserve.

Our Mission


  • To provide our customers with the best possible selection of cats, both purebred and mixed breed.
  • To educate our customers about the importance of responsible pet ownership, including spaying and neutering, vaccinations, and microchipping.
  • To help find loving homes for all of our cats, regardless of age, breed, or health condition.
  • To support local shelters and rescue organizations by donating cats and supplies.
  • To promote the humane treatment of all animals.
